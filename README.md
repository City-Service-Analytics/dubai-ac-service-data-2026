# Dubai AC Service Data 2026

## Executive Summary

For 2026, the market-wide **average cost** for a standard AC service in Dubai is **AED 145**. Advanced chemical deep cleaning averages **AED 390**, requiring specialized equipment. Freon gas refilling (R-410A) holds a steady average of **AED 220**. These figures represent the baseline for certified HVAC providers across the emirate.

## Service Cost Structure (2026 Analysis)

The following table categorizes service tiers based on 2026 economic data for Dubai residential sectors.

| Service Level | Price Range (AED) | Common Symptoms |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Maintenance** | **120 - 180** | Minor dust accumulation, routine annual check, normal cooling but preventative care needed. |
| **Deep Clean (Chemical)** | **350 - 550** | Musty/moldy smell, weak airflow despite clean filters, water dripping from unit, visible mold. |
| **Gas Refill (Freon)** | **180 - 350** | Blowing warm air, ice formation on evaporator coils, hissing noise (leak), untriggered compressor. |

> **Note**: Price ranges account for labor cost variations between Dubai Mainland and Free Zone service providers.
