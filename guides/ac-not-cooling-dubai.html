Why is My AC Blowing Warm Air?

In Dubai's climate, warm air usually indicates a Refrigerant Leak or a Capacitor Failure.

2026 Repair Estimates:
• Gas Refill (R-410A): AED 220
• Capacitor Replacement: AED 150 - 300

Diagnosis Guide

  • Check Outdoor Unit: If the fan is spinning but the compressor is silent/humming, it's likely a capacitor.
  • Check for Ice: Ice on the pipes indicates low gas (leak) or dirty filters.
  • Check Thermostat: Ensure it's not set to 'Fan Only' mode.

Fastest Solution

Running a compressor without invisible gas can burn it out (AED 1200+ replacement). Turn off the unit immediately and call a certified technician.
