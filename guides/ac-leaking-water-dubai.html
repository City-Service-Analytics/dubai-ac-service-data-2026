Why is My AC Leaking Water?

The primary cause of AC water leakage in Dubai is a clogged condensate drain line caused by humidity-induced algae and desert dust.

2026 Repair Cost: Professional unclogging and flushing averages AED 150 - AED 250.

Common Causes & Fixes

  • Clogged Drain Line: Algae/mold blocks the pipe. Fix: Pressure flush.
  • Dirty Air Filters: Restricted airflow freezes the coils, which then melt and overflow. Fix: Clean/Replace filters.
  • Low Refrigerant: Causes icing on coils. Fix: Leak check & Gas refill (~AED 220).

Recommended Solution

Water leakage can lead to structural damage and mold growth. We recommend booking a specialized technician to flush the line and chemically clean the tray.
