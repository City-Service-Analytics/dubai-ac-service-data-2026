Ideal Service Schedule (2026)

For Dubai Residents, the golden rule is Quarterly Service (Every 3-4 Months) to combat sand accumulation and maintain efficiency.

Annual Calendar

March
Pre-Summer Deep Clean

Chemical clean of coils before peak heat hits. Essential.

July
Mid-Summer Filter Check

Quick check to ensure filters aren't clogged by summer dust storms.

November
Winter Performance Check

Inspect electrical parts and drainage before reducing usage.

Why Contract?

Providers prioritize Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) holders during July/August breakdowns. An AMC saves ~20% compared to paying per-visit.
